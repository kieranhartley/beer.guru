Ingredients of Beer

Beer is made from 4 different ingredients. Malted Barley, hops, water & yeast. We soak the barley to release sugars, add hops for a sharp flavour and then use yeast to convert the sugars in to alcohol. Water is the medium that this chemical reaction takes place inside.
