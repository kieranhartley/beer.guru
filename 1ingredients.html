Ingredients of Beer

Beer is made from 4 different ingredients. Malted Barley, hops, water & yeast.
We soak the barley to release sugars, add hops for a sharp flavour and then use yeast to convert the sugars in to alcohol.
Water is the medium that this chemical reaction takes place inside.

Water

Water is a very important part of the beer making process.
If your beer is 5% alcohol, then the remaining 95% is water.
The water must be filtered and treated before brewing.

Malted Barley

Barley is a grain, similar to wheat, oats or rice. It is full of starch.
The starch must be converted into sugars before we can create alcohol.
This break down of starch into sugars happens during a process called malting & mashing.

Hops

Hops are a flower from a vine plant. They are full of essential oils which give a crisp, bitter flavour.
Hops also have a natural antiseptic in their oils which helps to preserve the beer.

Yeast

Yeast is a tiny single cell organism that is part of the Funghus family.
Yeast eat sugars and create alcohol and carbon dioxide as their waste product.
